Street Photo Shoot: Your Story in the Rhythm of the City

Ready to create your own story to the rhythm of the city?

Want to see yourself as the hero of a stylish story unfolding against the backdrop of your favourite city?

A street photo shoot is an opportunity to capture your personality, your emotions and your unique connection to the urban space.

Forget about studio walls and strained smiles! We will create together with you sincere and atmospheric shots that will tell more about you than a thousand words.

about me

My works are not just images, they are moments frozen in time, filtered through the prism of personal perception.

I use film, vintage optics, experiment with hand printing and processing techniques to create unique images filled with soul and character.

Here you will find echoes of bygone eras mixed with a contemporary look. The texture of memories, light and shadow as tellers of silent stories.

I look for inspiration in the past, in nature, in people, in the little things that surround us. My goal is to create photographs that make you think, feel and see the world in a new.

The snapshots are like scraps of dreams assembled into a kaleidoscope of feelings.

Stages of work

  1. Introduction and discussion

    We start by discussing your ideas, goals, and preferences. It helps me understand what mood and style you want to convey in your photos.

  2. Concept development

    Based on our conversation, I'm developing a shooting concept, including location selection, image selection, and creating a mudboard (if necessary).

  3. Taking photos

    On the day of shooting, I create a comfortable and creative atmosphere so that you can relax and enjoy the process. We work together to capture the best moments.

  4. Photo selection and processing

    After shooting, I carefully select the best shots and begin professional processing to emphasize the beauty and expressiveness of each photo.

  5. Transfer of the finished material

    You get high-resolution photos ready for publication and printing.
